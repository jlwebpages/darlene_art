<span class="art_paragraph">

I’ve developed quite a collection of hand-painted papers - everything from gel prints to papers covered hastily with leftover paint and simple prints made by pulling up excess paint. As I was looking through these recently, I found many patterns and textures that inspired me to experiment more deeply with collage. This piece emerged - and to me it suggests areas that I photograph while I hike in my local landscape.
