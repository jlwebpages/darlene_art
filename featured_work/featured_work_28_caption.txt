<!-- Paragraph -->

<span class="art_paragraph">

I think about how I used to peer through the lens of a camera, carefully aligning and framing the view of the horizon in the distance,
aware of color and space and how all the features of the landscape coalesce. Now, I quickly compose on the phone screen, knowing I can
eventually zoom, crop, and adjust with ease. It’s easier, but is it better? Something else to think about.

</span>
