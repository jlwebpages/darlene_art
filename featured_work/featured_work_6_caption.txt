Accumulations

</span>


<!-- Dimensions -->

<span class="art_dimensions">

23 x 35 inches on 2-inch cradled wood panel

</span>


<!-- Paragraph -->

<span class="art_paragraph">

The hours fly by - and images from each day accumulate in sometimes unexplainable combinations. This piece is an assemblage of hand-painted papers, including painted cloth and woven paper. The more I look at this piece, the more I see, and I’m reminded of all the photos I’ve taken and landscapes I’ve experienced. I hope this happens for the viewer as well! 

</span>
