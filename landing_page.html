DARLENE LAGUNA
MIXED MEDIA ARTIST
Landscape inspires me to investigate perceptions of space, distance, and connection. Working in layers and without expectations for a specific outcome, I apply acrylic paint, collage, and elements of my photography to develop paintings on wood panel, canvas, and paper. I use texture, color, shape, and line to abstract and suggest depth and a sense of place. Thank you for taking a look at my work.
