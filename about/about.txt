<span class="about_title">ARTIST STATEMENT</span>

<p>

<span style="font-style: italic; padding; 0px">“This is what it feels like to be here.”</span>

<p>

I create mixed-media works that blend collage, original photography, and acrylic paint to explore my
connection to the natural world. Inspired by the diverse landscape of southern California, I transform
my encounters into layered compositions that evoke memory, sensation, and place. I carefully select
materials that reflect the variety of perceptions I experience in my surroundings, using line, shape,
color, and texture to define and highlight the unique features of my experiences. My goal is to convey
a distinct sense of presence and beauty and to capture the unexpected.

<p>

<span class="about_title">BIO</span>

<p>

Darlene Laguna, a mixed-media artist based in Orange, California, is influenced by her engineering
background, which instilled in her a sense of precision, logic, and analytical thinking. However, it
was her love for hiking and exploring the natural world that ignited a profound sense of wonder that
led her to art-making. What started as landscape photography evolved into a unique mixed-media practice
that transforms her outdoor experiences into vibrant, layered, abstract compositions. Primarily self-taught,
Laguna employs photography, collage, and acrylic painting techniques to investigate the emotional resonance
of nature. Her work has been shown in numerous juried exhibitions and galleries.
